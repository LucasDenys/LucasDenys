# 💫 About Me:
Computer science student passionate about the genesis of artificial intelligence and the bio-inspired modeling of adaptive, cognitive, and morphological dynamics.<br><br>I am currently conducting research (Repositories & prototypes in development)on the emergence of intrinsically motivated agents, multimodal cognitive systems, and computational morphogenesis. My goal is to understand how an artificial agent can self-organize, develop its own internal models, evaluate its uncertainty, regulate its learning, interact socially, and generate novel structures, forming the basis of an ecology of adaptive and creative intelligences.<br><br>_____________________________<br><br>Research interests<br><br>Computer vision, intrinsic motivation, uncertainty modeling, multimodal cognition, embodied AI, bio-inspired complexity, learning–memory–language emergence, generative morphology, evolutionary systems, computational creativity, cognitive robotics.
